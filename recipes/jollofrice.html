Jollof Rice Recipes

This is cameroon style Jollof Rice

Image is courtesy of Keng's Kitchen a cameroonian food blogger

This recipe is very easy to follow and so yummy.Jellof or Jollof rice is popular amongst west africans.

Ingredients

  • 6 Cups Parboiled Rice
  • 6 large fresh tomatoes puree
  • 1 (oz) Canned Tomato Paste
  • 3 Red Bell Peppers Puree
  • 4 Medium Carrots Chopped
  • 2 Cups Green Beans Chopped
  • ½ Teaspoon Curry Powder
  • ½ Teaspoon White Pepper
  • ½ Teaspoon Anise
  • 1 Cup Cooking Oil
  • 1 Pound Beef Cubes
  • Bouilion to taste

Steps:

  1. Prepare the base: Heat oil in a pot and sauté onions until soft. Add minced garlic and ginger. Stir for a minute.
  2. Add the tomato mix: Pour in blended tomatoes, tomato paste, and bell peppers. Cook on medium heat until the tomato mix reduces and thickens, about 10-15 minutes.
  3. Season: Add thyme, curry powder, salt, and pepper to the tomato mix. Stir well.
  4. Add the stock: Pour in the chicken or vegetable stock, bring to a boil, and let it simmer.
  5. Cook the rice: Add washed rice to the pot. Stir to combine with the sauce. Reduce heat, cover, and cook for 20-30 minutes, or until the rice absorbs all the liquid and is tender.
  6. Serve: Fluff the rice and serve with your choice of meat or vegetables.

Enjoy your delicious Jollof rice
