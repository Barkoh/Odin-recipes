Poulet DG Recipe

Description

Poulet DG is a delicious and flavorful Cameroonian dish made with chicken, plantains, and vegetables. It’s perfect for family gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 4 ripe plantains (peeled and sliced)
  • 2 carrots (sliced)
  • 1 green bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 chicken bouillon cube

Steps

  1. Season the chicken pieces with thyme, white pepper, salt, and garlic. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and fry the chicken until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. Fry the plantain slices in the same oil until golden, then set aside.
  4. In the same pot, sauté the onions, carrots, and bell peppers until soft.
  5. Add the fried chicken back into the pot along with the bouillon cube. Stir well.
  6. Add a small amount of water, cover, and let it cook for about 20 minutes.
  7. Finally, add the fried plantains to the pot and mix gently. Cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
